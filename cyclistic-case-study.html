Cyclistic Bike-Share Case Study

Objective: Understand how casual riders and annual members use Cyclistic bikes differently to help design a conversion strategy.

Tools: R, Tableau, Excel

Steps Taken

  • Data cleaning and preparation using R
  • Exploratory analysis and visualization in Tableau
  • Identified peak usage times, ride durations, and membership patterns

Key Insights

  • Members ride more frequently but shorter distances
  • Casual riders prefer weekends and tourist-heavy months
  • Offering weekend membership promos could increase conversions
